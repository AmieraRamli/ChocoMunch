Our Story

ChocoMunch Enterprise is a partnership business focused on the production of chocolate jars. This company is run by a partnership of four people: the General Manager, the Marketing Manager, the Operations Manager, and the Finance Manager. ChocoMunch Enterprise's main target customers are those with a sweet tooth, children, and the younger generation. Our company creates one-of-a-kind chocolate jar snacks with a variety of toppings. ChocoMunch Enterprise was formed to express ideas for creating more unique chocolate jars in the Malaysian snack industry. ChocoMunch's creation allows consumers to try out new ways of tasting food.
